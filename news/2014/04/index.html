        • AkashaCMS v0.3.0 released - major rearchitecting, plugins, improvements, much more planned for v0.4.x I'm pleased to announce that AkashaCMS has reached version 0.3.0. This version has been over a year in development and isn't quite what I'd intended, but it is a result of the actual needs during the past year. The primary change was to architect AkashaCMS to support plugins, a move which allowed the creation of several useful plugins.
